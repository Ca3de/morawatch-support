Privacy Policy for Morawatch

Last updated: September 7, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Morawatch ("we," "our," or "the app") handles your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Morawatch collects minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Login Credentials: Your username and password for your personal media server (stored securely in iOS Keychain)
  • Server URL: The address of your personal media server (stored locally on device)
  • Watch Progress: Your viewing history and playback positions (stored locally and synced with your server)
  • App Preferences: Theme selection and other settings (stored locally on device)

How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you with your personal media server
  • Stream content from your media server
  • Track and sync your viewing progress
  • Save your app preferences for a personalized experience
  • Maintain your session while using the app

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties.

All communication occurs directly between:

  • The Morawatch app on your device
  • Your configured personal media server

No data passes through our servers or any third-party services.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected through:

  • iOS Keychain: Sensitive information like passwords are encrypted using iOS Keychain
  • Local Storage: Preferences and non-sensitive data stored locally on your device
  • Secure Communication: Support for HTTPS connections to your media server
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any of your data on cloud servers

Third-Party Services

Morawatch does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Morawatch does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app requires server credentials that typically only adults would possess. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You manually log out (clears stored credentials)
  • You delete the app (removes all local data)
  • You clear app data in Settings

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all stored data
  • Portability: Your media server retains your watch history
  • Correction: Update your credentials at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since Morawatch does not collect, sell, or share personal information with third parties, these rights are inherently protected.

European Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, Morawatch complies with GDPR requirements:

  • Minimal data collection (data minimization)
  • Clear purpose for data use
  • Secure data storage
  • User control over data
  • No data sharing with third parties
